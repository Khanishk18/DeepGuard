Deepfake Threat Landscape

Deepfakes are increasingly used in misinformation, impersonation, fraud, and social engineering. As generation quality improves, reliable and ethical detection becomes critical to protect public trust.

Misinformation

Manipulated media can sway public opinion and erode confidence in institutions and journalism.

Identity Fraud

AI-cloned voices and faces can bypass weak verification, enabling scams and account takeovers.

Cybersecurity

Social-engineering attacks now integrate synthetic voices in spear-phishing and voice-spoofing.

Societal Harm

Harassment, extortion, and reputational damage increase as tools become more accessible.
