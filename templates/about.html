About DeepGuard

DeepGuard is a multimodal deepfake detection platform that analyzes images and audio using deep learning. We combine CNN-based vision with Mel-spectrogram audio modeling to identify synthetic artifacts and cross-modal inconsistencies.

Real-time predictions
Image CNN (MobileNetV2)
Audio CNN on Mel-spectrograms
Privacy-friendly processing
Confidence scoring
Modular architecture

Our Mission

We aim to restore trust in digital media by offering a fast, accurate, and accessible detector that empowers journalists, platforms, and everyday users to verify content authenticity before sharing or acting on it. DeepGuard is built for extensibility: video, lip-sync alignment, and adversarial robustness are on our roadmap.

How It Works

  • Visual: Preprocessing + MobileNetV2 learns manipulation cues like unnatural edges, textures, and lighting.
  • Audio: We convert speech to Mel-spectrograms and train a CNN to detect synthetic frequency/time patterns.
  • Inference: Unified interface returns a label (Real/Fake) with a confidence bar for clarity.
